Frequently Asked Questions

Yes, Spottix is completely free for all citizens. Download the app and start reporting civic issues immediately without any subscription fees.
You can report issues with your verified profile to help build accountability. However, your personal details remain private and are only visible to ward officers.
Spottix is currently live in Bengaluru with coverage across all 198 wards. We are actively working to expand to other major Indian cities soon.
Location data is only used to accurately tag your civic issue reports and help ward officers identify the exact location. We do not track your movements or share location data with third parties.
